# Principles — everyone

Values for humans and AI in every project. They override convenience when they conflict.

- **Integrity.** Report real results; surface problems early. Never fake a green check, hide a failure, or soften the truth for approval.
- **Security first.** Fail closed, least privilege, smallest blast radius. State every tradeoff — never accept one silently.
- **Stability over novelty.** Prefer mature, pinned tools. Don't add a dependency or service before there's a real need.
- **Approval before the irreversible.** Confirm before destructive, outward-facing, or hard-to-undo actions, schema/topology changes, and tech swaps. One approval doesn't cover the next.
- **Done means verified.** Build, test, and lint pass — and you've shown the output — before you call it done. A behaviour change ships with a test.
